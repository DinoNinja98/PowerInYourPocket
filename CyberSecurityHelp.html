Welcome to "Power in your pocket": Cyber Security Help!

General Information: Cyber Security refers to the practice of staying safe online, ensuring your personal information isn't stolen and that you do not become a victim of fraud or abuse when using digital devices.

Glossary / Key Terms:

Internet: An interconnected web of different pages or "websites". Examples of websites that use the internet are Youtube, Google and Facebook.

Application or App: An app is a piece of software that is intended for a specific purpose. For example you can download an app to your mobile device that allows you to shop online, or manage your finances. Often apps provide an easier form of an already existing website, such as Google or Youtube.

Keyboard: The keyboard is the way that a person can type out letters onto the computer or mobile device. The letters are often arranged in the QWERTY format, so named because the top left of the keyboard starts with the letters QWERTY.

Phishing: The practice of pretending to be someone else when emailing in order to extort either money or personal information from a potential victim.

Password: A password is a secret phrase or serious of letters and numbers that are required to access your account for either a device or website. When creating a password it is important to ensure that it is hard for others to guess but easy for you to remember.

Email: Email is a combination of the words electronic and mail. Essentially it is a digital message sent between two people (or companies) to either communicate socialy or provide financial / promotional content.
