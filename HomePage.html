Welcome!

Thank you for visiting Power in your pocket. From my point of view, the power in your pocket on most days is your mobile phone or tablet. The way you can maximise your power is by using that device to the fullest.

To enhance your digital literacy education (or computer knowledge) there are a variety of educational resources found on the site.

In the Text Based Help section, you will find simplistic tips on the basics of mobile phones.

The Interactive tool section is the main way to use this site. You will be greeted by an introductory short video explaining how to use the tool, and with three options below. Simply select the desired option and you will be greeted by another short video with 3 further options. Continue progressing for as long as you are comfortable, and feel free to rewind or pause if at any point you need a break.

The Cyber Security Help section provides a kind of glossary of key cyber security terms you may encounter online. I wish you the best of luck in your digital literacy journey. Thank you for trying out my tool. Feel free to explore and make the most of your digital devices with "Power in your pocket"!
